DANGER!

Behind this sign lies a (pathetic) attempt at a zombie shooting
game, demonstrating Ecstasy Motion physics and AI Behaviors.

As you will quickly notice, it is not at all finished. The zombies frequently
get stuck, and you often can't shoot them for some reason. We plan to
improve this sample game and add more demos as time allows.

As it is, you'll have to try pretty hard to get yourself killed. If you do,
however, just hit the space bar or left mouse and you'll start over.
