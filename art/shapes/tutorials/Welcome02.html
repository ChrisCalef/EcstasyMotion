Sequences
 
 
Ecstasy Motion began as a tool for tweaking motion capture data.
  
With the Sequence tools, you can easily import large numbers of
animations, quickly crop out looping cycles, and then use your new
motions to build more complex scenes involving multiple actors.
  
You can modify sequences using the Keyframe Editing panel,
and input motion directly via Kinect or an OptiTrack mocap rig.
