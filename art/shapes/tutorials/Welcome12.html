BVH Support

As the most popular early format for motion capture, still in wide
use today, there are many thousands of BVH files available online.

Ecstasy allows batch import and exort of BVH files by directory.

You can set up BVH profiles mapping your own model to any given BVH
skeleton. You only have to do this once.

Whole scenes can be exported as connected sets of BVH files, and imported
into BVH-consuming apps such as Second Life, Daz3D, and many more.
