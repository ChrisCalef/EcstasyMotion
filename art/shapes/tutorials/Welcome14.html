T3D MIT

Ecstasy Motion was built as a plug-in for the extremely powerful
World Editor tool in Torque. In addition to the tools Ecstasy provides,
stock T3D offers environment features like Sky, Terrain, Water, Forests,
Road & River editors, Particle systems, Camera paths, and much more.

Torque has always been a great engine, but with the exciting release
of T3D 1.2 under the MIT Open Source License, the sky is now officially
the limit for the creative work that can be done with Torque.

Many users may wish to take advantage of these capabilities and capture
their final footage from within Ecstasy Motion itself.
