FBX Support

 
Ecstasy Motion allows export and (somewhat limited) import of FBX files.

Exports from Ecstasy can be used in the Unity3D game engine, modeling
applications such as 3DS Max and Maya, and many other destinations.

FBX scenes can be exported as pure animation (skeletons only) or with
meshes included. Actors can be saved out individually, or whole scenes
can be baked together to allow for easy repositioning in the final app.
