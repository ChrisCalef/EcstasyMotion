Physics

 

 

In addition to sequence editing and motion capture input, Ecstasy Motion uses
physics (PhysX) to generate animations unique to a model and its environment.
 
You can turn on debug rendering to see the PhysX thread's internal
representation of all the physics shapes and joints in the scene.
 
Although currently dependent on PhysX, Ecstasy's physics layer was
written to be easily extensible to other physics libraries, like bullet.
