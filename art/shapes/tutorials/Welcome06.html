Destructible Objects & Actors

 

 

In addition to joints and ragdoll physics, you can also record physics-based
animations for objects that come apart completely.
 
Every movable object in Ecstasy Motion is considered an Actor, and all actors
can record their motions into new sequences at any time.
 
An actor could be a human, but it could also be a hill covered with tumbling
boulders, or a collapsing building, or an exploding door or window.
