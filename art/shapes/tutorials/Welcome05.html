Weapons and Attacks

 

Using the Weapons panel, you can attach models to your actors at whatever
position and orientation you desire.

 

Weapons can be associated with any number of Weapon Attacks, which link a
specific animation with an amount of damage, a minimum and maximum range,
and a start and stop frame for the actual strike when damage is applied.
 

Behavior Trees can choose attacks based on range and other criteria.
