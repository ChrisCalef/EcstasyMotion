Scenes and Scene Events

 

The basic unit of organization in Ecstasy Motion is the Scene.
A scene consists of one or more actors, each with some combination of:

Playlists, Scene Events, and AI Behaviors
Scene Events are the glue that holds most scenes together. Scene Events
are scheduled events that can trigger physics actions, begin sequences,
call script functions or start AI behaviors.

  In this scene, the right and left zombies receive ragdoll force events,
while the center zombie has right and left torque forces on his head, and
